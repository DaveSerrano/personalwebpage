Sobre mim

I had the oportunity and the pleasure to work in different areas in Aviation Industry and to work and learn with many excellent professionals with such a range of expertise in different roles.

In aviation I've worked for different national airlines, based in different cities in Portugal and also international airlines with my previous experience flying Boeing 767. As a Pilot, I've been working on ferry flights, sightseeing flights and as a flight Instructor (VFR and IFR). Flying B767, I had the oportunity to operate over Europe, America, Asia and Africa not only schedule flights but also charter flights, ACMI and cargo flights.

I'm currently working as a Certified Flight Instructor in Cascais - LPCS (Lisbon area) conducting mainly IFR Instruction Flights on SEP airplanes.
